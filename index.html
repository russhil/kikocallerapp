About Us

We Built Kiko for Businesses Like Yours

A small, focused team solving a very real problem — phone orders that get lost, misheard, or forgotten.

Kiko AI was born from a simple observation: millions of Indian businesses still take orders over the phone — but writing them down by hand leads to errors, missed items, and lost customers.

We asked: what if every phone order automatically became a clean, confirmed WhatsApp message — without the seller lifting a pen?

That's Kiko. An AI that listens to call recordings, understands the order, and delivers it to WhatsApp — ready to send, ready to confirm.

We're a small team who've watched family members run food businesses and struggle with phone orders. Kiko is our way of giving every local business the same tools big companies have — without complexity or cost.

We're constantly improving. Every piece of feedback shapes what we build next.
