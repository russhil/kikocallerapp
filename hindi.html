FAQ

Aapke Sawaal, Hamare Jawab ❓

Download karne se pehle jo bhi jaanna chahte hain — sab yahan hai.

Kya Kiko mere phone calls record karta hai?
Nahi. Kiko khud calls record nahi karta. Yeh sirf un call recordings ko process karta hai jo aapke phone pe already save hoti hain — aapke device ke built-in call recorder ya kisi third-party recorder se.
Kya Kiko ke liye internet chahiye?
Internet sirf tab chahiye jab AI aapki recording process kare aur WhatsApp pe order bheje. Actual phone call aapke normal mobile network pe kaam karta hai — koi VoIP ya internet calling nahi chahiye.
Kiko kaun si languages support karta hai?
Abhi Hindi, English, aur Hinglish (mixed) support hoti hai. Aage Tamil, Telugu, Marathi aur Bengali bhi aa rahi hain jaldi.
Kya mera data safe hai?
Haan. Sab data encrypted hai (TLS/HTTPS transit mein, at rest bhi). Audio files real-time mein process hoti hain aur servers pe store nahi rehti. Aap kabhi bhi apna account aur data delete kar sakte hain.
Kya meri staff bhi Kiko use kar sakti hai?
Business plan pe aap multiple staff accounts set up kar sakte hain. App itna simple hai ki non-tech staff bhi bina training ke use kar le.
Kaunse phones pe Kiko kaam karta hai?
Koi bhi Android phone jo Android 8.0 ya usse upar ho. Koi special hardware nahi chahiye — bas aapka regular smartphone.
Kya main kabhi bhi subscription cancel kar sakta hoon?
Bilkul. App se ya help@kiko.live pe email karke kabhi bhi cancel karein. Koi lock-in nahi, koi sawaal nahi.
Kya koi free version hai?
Haan! Kiko free mein shuru hota hai. Play Store se download karo, 2 minute mein setup karo, aur turant orders capture karna shuru karo. Koi credit card nahi chahiye.
